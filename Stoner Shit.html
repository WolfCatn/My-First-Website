420

I am definitely a stoner, I love smoking weed and getting high. I mean let's be real though, who doesn't like getting high?

Here are some stoner tips:

  • Always have a drink nearby for cottonmouth when smoking.
  • There's levels to being high.
  • Eating mangos is supposed to help boost your high.
  • If you follow up a hit with a vape hit, it helps it not hurt as much.
  • If you need to grind some weed, but don't have a grinder, use an empty pill bottle and a nickel.
  • You can make a pipe from an apple or even a potato.
  • If you spread a light coat of honey on your joints, it helps them burn slower and more evenly.
  • If you're a music lover like me, create a Spotify Stoner Mix. Music is amazing when you're high.
  • Use a sock wrapped around a vacuum hose to pick up spilled weed, as the weed gets stuck in the sock and doesn't get sucked into the vacuum.
  • Smoke before you eat, food will taste better.
  • If you use frozen fruit in your bong water, it gives it flavor.
  • You can use pipe cleaners to clean your pipes.
  • Just superglue a magnet to your bong, and then you can have your lighter attached to your bong.
  • If you need to hide weed, hide it in the bottom of a deodorant container.
  • Use a paper towel roll and dryer sheets to hide the scent of the weed smoke. Put the dryer sheets in the roll and blow through it.
  • You can use scissors instead of a grinder.
  • Have a stoner kit for after smoking. Make sure to have eye drops, sunglasses, a drink, and mints/gum.
  • Do not go to the store high, you will probably overspend.
  • If you enjoy watching shows while stoned, prepick the show so when you're high you don't have to worry about picking a show.
  • Know the strain and how it affects you. Some strains are more energizing, while others are more relaxing.
I hope you find this information helpful! If you have more advice I could add, feel free to let me know!
